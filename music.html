Music

Regular Performers

A lot of great talents have graced our stage since we’ve expanded the club’s footprint. This includes our house band, Lionfish. These four lovely androids have been with us since the opening and you can find them performing most nights, often Mondays through Thursdays.

Another regular presence is the incomparable DJ Greendown. DJ Greendown is known around town for hosting our Friends & Anemones nights, but he can be found DJing here on the weekends he’s not scheduled in the kingdoms.

Upcoming Shows

Special guests who will be on the schedule soon include superstar Cindi Mayweather, the 75s, and East Providence locals, Electric Sheep. Check the calendar for more details.
