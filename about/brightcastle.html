History of Brightcastle

Brightcastle and East Providence were originally built as expansion cities for Tafota to the west and southwest of the kingdom. East Providence operated as a stopover along the route to kingdoms further west, and Brightcastle served as a trading port along the Aurora River. Brightcastle was also a hub for android manufacturing, but was destroyed by sonic bombing during the People's Revolt. In the decades since the bombing, it has been rebuilt as the Glasslands.

The Glasslands

After the Peopleâ€™s Revolt, the entire area was covered in shards of glass. Tafota decided not to officially rebuild the city, instead leaving the remaining inhabitants to fend for themselves. Over decades of slow recovery, the glass was never fully cleared and became embedded into the grass and roadways. When looking down at the area from the mountains, the reflection off the glass causes the city appear as a shimmering part of river it neighbors. Thus, the city was unofficially renamed to the Glasslands.

No longer a port city, it is now a rest area for travellers and any who make it through the Rust. As a Bluelight District, it is considered a safe haven for cyborgs and androids who fled or were exiled from Tafota and other nearby kingdoms.
