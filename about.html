About Us

The Owner

Wil Weekes is a G5 android who was commissioned 8 years ago. He was originally a factory cook who helped serve workers helping to rebuild the Glasslands. When major construction projects were completed three years after he started working, Wil moved on to open his own small restaurant in the newly reconstructed block.

Willy's in the Glasslands

Originally named Willy’s, his turquoise and orange-colored storefront became known for his rotating selection of pizzas, burgers, and stirfrys. While he regularly came up with his own signature dishes, he prided himself on keeping his menu a mystery. His logic: if customers didn’t know what to expect, they’d be more likely to return again to see what was new or if their favorite had returned. And it worked.

After two years of great success, Wil decided to revise and expand the business. He put a deposit on a section of a developing retail block along the east bank of the river. He decided to keep the main floor as a restaurant and add a club venue in the basement. He had met many people moving into the Glasslands or just passing through, and wanted to give them the ability to share their talents. He hired Darius Hunter as his booker and promoter and business has been booming along the Riverwalk ever since.
