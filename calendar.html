Monthly Events

First of the month: $1 wells. $1 shots. $1 classic pizza slices. $1 basket of regular fries. Replaces Margarita Mondays or Waffle Wednesdays if they coincide.

Third Weekend of Every Month: Friends & Anemones nights on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. All androids get into the club free or get 25% off your main floor bar tab when they bring in a cyborg or human. One discount type per party per day.

Margarita Mondays: $4 margaritas. $7 whole 11” thin-crust margherita pizzas.

Waffle Wednesdays: We’re in ze cloob. With waffles fries and waffle sticks. It doesn’t have to make sense. Sometimes we just want waffles and ridiculous beats, my friends.
