Food and Drink Menu

Ask us about our classic menu when you come in! Here are some of the specialty items featured at clearwaters.

Pizza (11”)

All of our classic pizzas come with hand-tossed crusts and tomato sauce.

Rashni: Our original specialty pizza, the Rashni is a thin-crust pie with a spread of creamy garlic sauce. There’s no layer of mozzarella, so that garlicky goodness directly embraces grilled chicken, mushrooms, spinach, and sprinkles of crumbled goat cheese.

Nora: Our newest specialty pizza is the Nora. It starts with a hand-tossed crust and a thin layer of tomato sauce. Like her sister before her, this pie foregoes the mozzarella for a few handfuls of parmesan on top of prosciutto, mizuna, and cherry tomatoes.

Burgers

Willy: Quarter pound beef patty, bacon, avocado, thousand island dressing, grilled onions, and swiss cheese on a sesame bun.

Sides

Rustbelt: A mixed basket of regular, sweet potato, and curly fries topped with ground beef, cheddar, and grilled onions.

Cocktails

Seawater: A green tea mojito with lime and muddled mint.
