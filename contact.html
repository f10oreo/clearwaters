Directions

From East Providence: Use the Atlanta borough gate and travel down Route 41. At the fork, continue left to stay on Route 41 and take it all the way to the Glasslands. Inside the district, take a slight right to stay on Main Street, then turn right onto Riverwalk. We’re the fifth door up on the block.

From the North or South: From the North, get to Port Shibal. From the South, get to Port Hyde. Any captain in the either marina will take you to the Glasslands for a small fee. From the Glasslands marina, cross the bridge to the east bank and take a right onto Riverwalk. We’ll be two blocks down.

From Tafota: If you somehow find yourself leaving Tafota, the best way to leave is from the Washington borough. Position yourself behind Tarvarez Hall with your back to the kingdom wall, and take a straight path ahead through the rustbelt. You'll travel through the narrowest part of the rustbelt to a decon station where someone will meet you to take you into the Glasslands.
