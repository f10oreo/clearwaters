History of Brightcastle

Brightcastle was built as a expansion city in the valley southwest of the Rust. It was a major port and hub for android manufacturing serving Tafota and East Providence, but was destroyed by sonic bombing during the People's Revolt. In the decades since the bombing, it has been rebuilt as the Glasslands.

The Glasslands

After the sonic bombs, the entire area was covered in shards of glass. Tafota decided not to rebuild the city, instead leaving the remaining inhabitants to fend for themselves. Over the decades, the glass became embedded into the grass and roadways. When looking down at the area from the mountains during certain times of the day, the city seems to disappear and reflect light as if it were part of the river it neighbors. Thus, the city was unofficially renamed to the Glasslands.

No longer a river port city, it is now a rest area for travellers and any who make it through the Rust. As a Bluelight District, it is considered a safe haven for cyborgs and androids who fled or were exiled from Tafota and other nearby kingdoms.
