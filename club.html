Club Rules

Androids and Cyborgs only. Humans must be accompanied and vouched for by a blue to gain admittance.

Minors are allowed on the main floor until 9pm. Organics 18+ years of age (Androids: 3+) are allowed on both floors until 10:30pm. Alcohol service still follows kingdom standard: 21+/4+ only.

Casual dress code for the main floor restaurant with shirts, shorts, and shoes required. To get into the downstairs club, you have to put a little more effort into your outfit. You can keep your jeans, but leave your distressed pairs at home. No flip flops and no ratty sneakers.

Tickets

There’s no admittance fee for the main floor, but the fee for the downstairs club is $8 per person. Wristbands must be bought at the counter by the stairs. On days with special guests, wristbands are $15 and sales stop an hour before showtime.

Operating Hours

Main Floor: 12pm - 12am. Orders for the kitchen end at 11:30pm and bar tabs can be moved downstairs.

Downstairs: 5pm - 2am. Last call for the bar is at 1:30am. Tabs that aren’t closed by the end of the night will have 5% of their total added for each day the tab remains open.
