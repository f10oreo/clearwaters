D'Cruz Cafe

The D’Cruz is run by Mads Weekes. Mads is a G4 android has been in the Glasslands since the bombing of Brightcastle. If you want to know more about the area or get to know the neighbors, he’s the android to ask.

D’Cruz is still a small, hole-in-the-wall cafe even though it’s been around for a decade, but it’s full almost every minute that its open. The atmosphere is calm, and the company is lovely.

Mads runs a small cafe in East Providence as well, so the Glasslands location is only open Monday through Thursday morning. You can find the cafe at 16 Riverwalk.
