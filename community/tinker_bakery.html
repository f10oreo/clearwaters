Tinker Bakery

Tinker is a bakery Run by Minerva Weekes. Ervy is also a G4 android, but was commissioned five years after Mads. She opened Tinker seven years ago, but moved locations last year. Now Tinker is our next door neighbor! Look for the yellow door sandwiched between clearwaters and D’Cruz.

The bakery shares a patio with D’Cruz so grab a coffee and one of Tinker’s signature G4 cupcakes (Congo chocolate with vanilla buttercream and toffee) before enjoying the river view.

Like Mads, Ervy runs a second location in East Providence. The Glasslands location is open Tuesday afternoon through Friday. The bakery is located at 17 Riverwalk.
